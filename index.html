What AM I do with my life?

As I approach the final year of my twenties, I ask myself what every other young adult on the cusp of 30 inevitably asks, "What the hell am I doing with my life??" Well, I thought about it, and this is what I came up with:

  • Aerials
  • Mermaiding
  • Contortion
  • Pole
  • This and That

Learning to Fly

Did you know you can run away and join the circus here in St.Louis? Our very own City Museum hosts Circus Harmony, where you can run away to and join the circus and still be home for dinner. These stills are from a recent microclass--my first time doing aerials in over a year!

Under The Sea

Through a chance workplace meeting, I became friends with the head of the local Missori Mermaids pod. Prior to the pandemic, we met up for pod swims and picnics once a month. Eagerly awaiting the day we can swim together again!.

Pretzel Time

I am naturally hypermobile due to having Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome, but I didn't know how to develop that flexibilty until I began taking contortion classes at Circus Harmony. I may not be able to pretzel just yet, but I did finally achieve a lifelong goal: to do a backbend from standing. Next mission: Stand out of a backbend!

Pole Position

I began my pole journey with another St Louis gem: Mynx Academy of Pole Dance
"I'll never forget the day my pole came in the mail--it was the day after my boyfriend died in a motorcycle accident. I may not be great at any of the endeavors I take on, but I have to keep living and doing, to honor his memory.
